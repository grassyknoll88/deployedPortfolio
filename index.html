About Me

Who Am I?

I am a full stack web developer, with experience in furniture design. As a maker, I have drafted blueprints and implemented manufacturing processes from start to finish, while maintaining the efficiency and versatility demanded by the project at hand. I believe this unique skill-set partnered with the combination of my creativity, detail orientation, and years of customer service will result in great success within the software industry. Through these experiences I have developed a passion to create clean and responsive user-experiences.

Currently, I am attending the University of Kansas Full Stack Flex Program in the hopes of transitioning into a new career field that offers an inventive and collaborative work environment which will utilize my desire to build and create. My hobbies include rooting for the Kansas City Royals, traveling, taking my dogs, Snoopy and Clyde, to the dog park, playing video games, and binge watching 90's television shows with my lovely wife.

University of Kansas - Full Stack Flex Program

The program curriculum is designed with the most in-demand market needs in mind. The KU Full Stack Flex Program empowers expert instructors to deliver a dynamic learning experience. Students are immersed in an intensive educational experience including a broad array of technologies such as: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Java, Bootstrap, Express.js, React.js, Node.js, Database Theory, Bookshelf.js, MongoDB, MySQL, Command Line, and Git. Students not only learn the fundamentals, but also apply their knowledge to solve real-world problems, and build portfolios. The KU Full Stack Flex Program is designed for professionals and students who are actively pursuing a career change or advancement or are looking to gain a new skill set.

2015

Center for Furniture Craftsmanship

The mission of the Center for Furniture Craftsmanship is to provide the best possible education for people who want to design and build functional, beautiful, and expressive work out of wood to the highest standard of craftsmanship. At the heart of the Center’s mission is the belief that design and craftsmanship are deeply meaningful expressions of the human spirit. They contribute to individual fulfillment and inform the larger culture. Our programs are for novice, intermediate, and advanced woodworkers who seek an inspiring learning experience in a supportive environment.
